About Education Research Publications Experience Contact

About

My work examines how learners engage with emerging technologies, including generative AI, programming environments, multimodal learning analytics, and computational thinking activities. I am particularly interested in how human-AI interaction can support learners' thinking processes, problem solving, and interdisciplinary learning.
